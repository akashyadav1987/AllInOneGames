About Us/Mission Statement

We are the FlyCuts Team. An app driven to satisfy your needs, wants, desires, etc. in haircuts and hairstyles. Our overall goal is to give every person what they want in looking a certain way from haircuts to hairstyles. Also, our mission is to provide you the convenience of not having to leave your home or workplace. You have the luxury of choosing any of our partners, barber/stylist, to come to your home, office, etc. and give you the best quality & ideal haircut/style. As you know, just commuting to a salon or barbershop can take valuable time away from one’s life. We are here to save you that very time that you lose from commuting and waiting to be called for your turn for a haircut. 

With a tap of a button, you schedule any barber/stylist of your choice & they will come to you. All we ask of you, our customers, is to provide us with a chair, an outlet socket for clippers to get plugged in, and some space for our stylists/barbers to move around as they cut your hair. That’s it; you tell our stylist/barber what you like & let our partners handle the rest. It’s that easy. 

Lastly, all payments & tips will be done through the app, so you will never have to worry about cash. Convenient isn’t it!
